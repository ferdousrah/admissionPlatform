10 Affordable Student Cities in 5 Countries

Studying abroad can be expensive, but there are affordable options available. Here are 10 student-friendly cities in 5 different countries where you can live and study on a budget:

  • Germany: Berlin, Leipzig, Dortmund, Essen, Bochum
  • Canada: Montreal, Winnipeg, Halifax, Ottawa, Edmonton
  • Australia: Adelaide, Brisbane, Perth, Hobart, Canberra
  • Spain: Valencia, Granada, Seville, Bilbao, Salamanca
  • Italy: Bologna, Turin, Padua, Pisa, Naples

These cities offer affordable accommodation, transportation, and living expenses, making them ideal destinations for international students on a budget.
