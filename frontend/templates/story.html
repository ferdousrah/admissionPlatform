Our Story

 

The admissions platform simplifies the study abroad search, application, and acceptance process by connecting international students, placement partners, and academic institutions on one platform.

Founded in 2022 by Mustaq Ahmed, the admissions platform has partnered with leading colleges and universities in secondary, and post-secondary educational institutions, and works with several recruitment partners to diversify campuses across Australia, Canada, the US, the UK and Europe.

Partnering with top colleges and universities worldwide, including institutions in Australia, Canada, the US, the UK, and Europe, Admissions Platform offered a comprehensive array of study options. These partnerships, coupled with collaborations with secondary and post-secondary educational entities, ensured a diverse and vibrant campus experience.

Driven by a commitment to accessibility, Admissions Platform rapidly became the world's largest online platform for international student recruitment. Students benefitted from streamlined search, application, and acceptance processes, empowering them to pursue their academic aspirations with ease.

The team's dedication to innovation fueled the platform's growth, with continuous enhancements to features and functionality. Headquartered in Dhaka, Bangladesh, the platform served as a beacon for students worldwide, offering them a pathway to global education.

Looking ahead, Admissions Platform aimed to expand its network to new countries and regions, furthering its mission of democratizing access to education. With a team passionately dedicated to empowering students, the platform remained at the forefront of facilitating global education opportunities.
