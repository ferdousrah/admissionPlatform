All About Australia​

Are you thinking about studying in Australia? If so, you’re in for the adventure of a lifetime. Australia provides a world-renowned education and competitive work opportunities. Not to mention access to some of the world’s most breathtaking natural scenery and welcoming multicultural cities!

Getting an Australian Student Visa​

International students need a student visa (Subclass 500) to travel to and study in Australia.To apply, you’ll submit your student visa application online through the Australian Government’s ImmiAccount system. You can only apply for your Australian student visa after enrolling in a course of study, paying your enrollment deposit, and receiving your Electronic Confirmation of Enrollment (eCoE).

Proof of English Proficiency in Australia

To study in Australia, you’ll need one of the following English language proficiency test scores from within the past three years. Accepted tests and score requirements vary by course and institution, so check your course language requirements very closely.

TOEFL
IELTS
PTE
C1
OET

The Test of English as a Foreign Language (TOEFL) has been trusted by colleges and universities worldwide for over fifty years.

The International English Language Testing System (IELTS) is the most popular English language test in the world.

The Pearson Test of English (PTE Academic) measures your English reading, writing, speaking, and listening ability in a short two-hour test.

The Cambridge C1 Advanced exam, previously known as Cambridge English: Advanced (CAE), provides in-depth proof of language qualification.

The Occupational English Test (OET) is specifically for professionals in the healthcare sector.
